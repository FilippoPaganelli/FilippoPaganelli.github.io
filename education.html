  • Master's Degree in Computer Engineering
    Graduated from a 2-year Master's Programme in Computer Engineering at The University of Bologna. Third semester and thesis were completed at The University of Southern Denmark as an Erasmus+ exchange student.

    My thesis entails an Arduino-EMG sensor physical prototype that measures forearm rehabilitation exercises, during execution, and sends the data to a Flutter mobile app via BLE. The app is connected to a Node.js/React webapp hosted on Heroku, which serves API endpoints and interfaces with a MongoDB remote database. The overall project is a proof of concept of a technological solution that can be used to increase motivation in patients, by providing real-time and long-term visual feedback. As an additional visual representation, a robotics visualisation interfaces with the Arduino to show real-time contractions on a Simulink 3D model of the UB Hand IV, from the Laboratory of Automation and Robotics at The University of Bologna, using MATLAB with a BLE library.
